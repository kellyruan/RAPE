Keith testified in front of a grand jury and the perpetrator was indicted—but the the man responsible never stood trial. Before the trial date, the perpetrator was beaten to death by unknown assailants. “The real healing was achieved when I started to sincerely believe I’m not responsible for what happened to me.”
