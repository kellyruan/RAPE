Our mission

The goal of this page is to not only raise awareness of the silenced topic of sexual assault, but create a network in which survivors can share their experiences, and get support from other survivors. Our website is completely anonymous so that the identity of the user is hidden. We also hope to create a system in which college students can look at their school's specific webpage to post about suspicious people.
